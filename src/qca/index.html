Research Notes

  • Version 1 is intentionally still-frame only after cine review.
  • Semi-automatic segmentation works best when you place anchors through bends.
  • Border and stenosis editing are first-class controls, not a fallback.
  • Absolute millimeter values still depend on same-frame calibration quality and remain research-only.
  • Phase 2 is where branch handling and more robust vessel tracking should land.
