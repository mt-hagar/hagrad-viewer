Learning
No local reference-standard cases have been stored yet.

`Draw` traces a contour while you hold the mouse button. `Multiple Click` places contour points one by one; double-click to finish a smoothed contour.

`Adjust` refines handles or translates an existing contour. `Rubber` excludes unwanted pixels from the EAT count. `Let's Go Segment` uses your contoured slices as anchors, then `Copy To All Other Recons` transfers the completed contour set.

Shortcuts: `D` draw contour, `Q` multiple click, `E` adjust, `R` rubber, `O` overlay, `V` compare, `T` transfer, `X` clear contour, and `Shift+X` clear rubber.
