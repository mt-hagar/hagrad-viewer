HAGRad Advanced Research Prototype

HAGRad Contrast CalcScorer

Separate post-contrast calcification estimation prototype for coronary CTA and contrast-enhanced cardiac CT. Load a study, choose the best series, define the slab, pick reference ROIs, run a contrast-suppressed research estimate, manually edit lesions, and export per-vessel and total measurements without changing the existing HAGRad Viewer or the current noncontrast CalcScorer workflow.

Research-only. Standard Agatston scoring is defined on dedicated noncontrast calcium-scoring CT. On ordinary single-energy CTA, calcium and iodine overlap, so outputs here are post-contrast research estimates rather than validated noncontrast-equivalent calcium scores.

Review / Edit

Manual correction follows the estimate.

`Assign / Draw`: left click selects or adds, change the active category to relabel the selected lesion, right click removes, and left-drag draws an inclusion region. `Erase`: left click removes, and left-drag erases the drawn region. Manual overrides persist after re-estimation. Viewer handline shortcuts: `W` window, `M` pan, `Z` zoom, and right-mouse drag for fast slice scrolling.
