ROI Tools

`ROI Circle` starts from the initial click as the center. Drag outward to size it, release to create it, then left drag inside to move it or drag the single outer handle to resize it.

Right-click an existing circular ROI to show its edit state.

`ROI Brush` paints a threshold-aware continuous ROI. Hold the mouse button down to paint, then use `Grow +` or `Shrink -` to refine it.

`Eraser` uses a round brush. It trims ROI contours and brush ROIs, and removes other annotations it touches.

`ROI Draw` traces freehand while you hold the left mouse button, then closes when you release. `ROI Multiple Click` places discrete points and finishes on double-click, then smooths the final contour so it does not keep polygon edges.

Press `Space` after finishing a guided ROI to move to the next requested target.

`Adjust ROI` refines the selected ROI contour. Start on or just beside an existing ROI, it will auto-select it, then redraw the part you want to replace and release to blend the correction into the ROI.

Keyboard shortcut: `A`.

In stent mode, you can drag the colored guide lines on the curve to fine-tune the cutoffs manually. The measurements update automatically.
