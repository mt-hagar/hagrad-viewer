HAGRad NoiseLab

Standalone scientific workstation for exact square-ROI noise analysis in DICOM CT and other grayscale medical imaging. NoiseLab prioritizes pixel fidelity, explicit units, reproducible ROI geometry, and structured research export.

Built as a dedicated HAGRad scientific workflow for reproducible local noise characterization.

Tools

Mouse wheel scrolls slices. Cmd/Ctrl + wheel zooms. Middle-button drag pans. Right-drag fast-scrolls.

Keyboard: W = window/level, Z = zoom, M = move/pan, Esc = Select.

Square ROI

Drag on the CT image to place a square. Afterwards drag the ROI body to move it, or drag a corner to resize.

Pixel-square keeps equal pixel counts in x and y. Physical-square keeps equal edge length in millimeters and can become non-square in pixel counts when spacing is anisotropic.

NPS Rings

Choose NPS Rings, click the center once, move the mouse to size the circle, then double-click to place all square ROIs.
